# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback with professionalism.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to repository owners via GitHub.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
